About

Citizen OS is an e-decisionmaking platform for communities. It enables participative discussion and voting for groups, where getting everyone together into the same room oronlineatthe same time is not an option. Inhabitants of a village. Members of an NGO spread out all over the country. Employees of a multinational company from different cities and timezones.Spontaneous citizen movements. Anyone can start a discussion. E-democracy. E-meetings. E-assemblies. E-discussions. E-decisionmaking. Citizen OS - the choice is yours.
